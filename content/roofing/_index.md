# Florida Roofing Cost Guides

Florida roofing isn't like roofing anywhere else in the country. Between the High Velocity Hurricane Zone requirements in Miami-Dade and Broward, the FBC 8th Edition fastener schedules, and an insurance market that's dropping homes with roofs older than 15 years, what you pay for a roof here is driven by forces most national cost sites don't even mention.

Our roofing guides break down real costs by city — because a roof replacement in Fort Lauderdale (HVHZ, Miami-Dade NOA required) costs significantly more than the same job in Jacksonville or Tallahassee. Every guide includes material comparisons, labor rates for your specific market, permit costs and processes for your county, and the insurance implications that make roofing the most consequential home improvement decision in Florida right now.

Whether you're replacing asphalt shingles, upgrading to concrete or clay tile, or considering standing seam metal, start with the guide for your city below. Each one includes step-by-step contractor hiring advice and links to verify licenses at MyFloridaLicense.com.
