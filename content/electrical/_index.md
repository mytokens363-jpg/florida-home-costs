# Florida Electrical Cost Guides

Electrical work in Florida is tightly regulated — every project from a panel upgrade to a ceiling fan installation requires a permit and inspection. Florida's 200-amp panel upgrade demand has surged as homeowners add EV chargers, pool equipment, and whole-house generators. And in hurricane zones, electrical systems must meet additional wind resistance and water intrusion standards.

Our electrical guides provide installed pricing specific to your Florida market. A 200-amp panel upgrade in Miami runs $2,000-$4,000 compared to $1,500-$3,000 in North Florida — and the permit process varies significantly by county. We cover all of it: costs, permits, required contractor licenses (EC or ER), and what to expect from inspection.
