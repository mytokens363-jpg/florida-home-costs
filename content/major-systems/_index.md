# Florida Major Systems Cost Guides

Major system replacements are the projects homeowners dread — septic tank failures, foundation issues, whole-house insulation, mold remediation, and solar installations. These are five-figure projects that can't be postponed once they're needed, and Florida's climate, soil conditions, and building codes make every one of them different from the national norm.

Septic systems in Florida's high water table require different engineering than dry-soil states. Solar panel ROI is among the best in the country thanks to 250+ sunny days per year and net metering credits. Mold remediation is a year-round concern because Florida's humidity never truly lets up.
