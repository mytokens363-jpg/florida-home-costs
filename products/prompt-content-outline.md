## Quick Start Guide for Agents

1. **Choose Your Pack:** Select the prompts you need most
2. **Copy & Paste:** Copy any prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini
3. **Customize:** Add your specific details (location, price, features)
4. **Edit:** Refine the output to match your voice
5. **Use:** Copy the final text to your email, social media, or listing

## Pro Tips

- Save frequently used prompts in a notes app
- Create custom variations for different neighborhoods
- Keep a library of successful outputs for quick reference
- Update prompts quarterly based on what works best
