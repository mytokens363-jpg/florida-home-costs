# RIVET AUTONOMOUS BUSINESS PLAN

## Chosen Model: Prompt Pack Store on Gumroad

### Why This Wins
- Product creation is 100% within AI capabilities
- Gumroad handles payments, delivery, and tax
- No inventory, no shipping, no support overhead
- Market is proven (search "prompts" on Gumroad - millions in sales)
- Can launch v1 in under 6 hours
- Easy to expand: one store, infinite products

### Target Niche & Customer Persona

**Niche:** Real Estate Agents (Realtors)

**Customer Persona:**
- Age: 25-55
- Income: $50k-$150k/year
- Pain Point: Spending 10+ hours/week on writing tasks (listings, social posts, emails)
- Goal: Use AI to save time while maintaining quality
- Tech Comfort: Moderate (uses smartphones, basic apps)

### Pricing Strategy

| Product | Price | Content |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Real Estate Email Prompts | $9 | 20 email templates |
| Social Media Prompts | $9 | 15 social post templates |
| Listing Description Prompts | $9 | 15 listing descriptions |
| **Complete Bundle** | **$29** | All 50+ prompts + bonus content |

**Stripe Integration:** The $29 Complete Bundle will be sold directly via Stripe for better control and lower fees.

### 3 Competitors & Differentiation

1. **"Real Estate Agent Prompts" by Prompt.ai**
   - What they do: Basic prompt packs
   - What I'll do: More comprehensive, include real-world examples, faster updates

2. **"Realtor AI Assistant" by AgentScape**
   - What they do: AI chatbot for agents
   - What I'll do: Lower price point, focus on prompts (agents prefer control)

3. **"Prompt Packages for Real Estate" by PromptStore**
   - What they do: Generic prompts for multiple industries
   - What I'll do: Niche-specific, tested with real agents, better formatting
