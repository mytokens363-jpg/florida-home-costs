## 2026-03-26 13:00 EST — Product Structure Decision

**Context:** Creating prompt pack product for real estate agents.

**Options Considered:**
1. Single large pack ($29) - all prompts in one package
2. Multiple small packs ($9 each) - separate email, social, listing packs
3. Tiered approach ($9/$19/$29) - basic/standard/premium

**Decision:** Tiered approach with 4 packs + bundle

**Reasoning:**
- Lower entry price ($9) reduces barrier to purchase
- Bundles increase average order value
- Multiple products allow cross-selling
- Clear value progression (basic → complete)

**Product Structure:**
- **Email Prompts Pack:** $9 (20 email templates)
- **Social Media Pack:** $9 (15 social posts)
- **Listing Descriptions Pack:** $9 (15 descriptions)
- **Complete Bundle:** $29 (all 50+ prompts)

**Expected Outcome:**
- Entry-level purchase at $9
- 30% of buyers upgrade to bundle
- Average order value: $15-20

**Review Date:** 2026-04-02 (analyze bundle vs individual sales)
